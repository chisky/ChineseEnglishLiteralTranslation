Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2023
ChineseEnglishDict.com is an educational website that helps users learn Chinese expressions through literal translations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use data to improve our educational content and user experience.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our educational content. This helps us improve our website and content for better learning experiences.

Google Analytics collects the following information:

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information
  • Behavioral data: Pages visited, time spent on site, navigation patterns
  • Session data: Visit duration, bounce rate, traffic sources

Important: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or other identifying details. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymous data collected by Google Analytics exclusively for educational improvement purposes:

  • To identify which Chinese expressions and categories are most popular
  • To improve website navigation and user experience for learners
  • To optimize content organization and educational effectiveness
  • To understand how students interact with different learning materials

No personal data is used - All information is anonymous and aggregated to protect your privacy while helping us create better educational content.

3. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics (Tracking ID: G-42MM33VBV4): We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. This is our only third-party service. Google Analytics helps us understand:

  • Which Chinese expression categories are most visited
  • How users navigate through our educational content
  • Popular learning paths and content usage patterns
  • Device and browser preferences of our learners

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about your website usage. This information (including your IP address) is transmitted to Google's servers in the United States for processing.

How to opt-out: You can choose not to be tracked by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Alternatively, you can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls
  • Secure data storage

5. Your Rights and Choices

Since we only collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, your privacy rights are straightforward:

  • Opt-out of tracking: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disable cookies: Adjust your browser settings to block cookies
  • Delete browsing data: Clear your browser history and cookies
  • Browser privacy modes: Use private/incognito browsing

Note: We do not collect personal information, so there's no need to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data. However, if you have concerns about how we handle data, please contact us at jianchaochi@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

7. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted on servers located in the United States. If you are visiting from the European Union or other regions with laws governing data collection and use, please note that your information may be transferred to, and processed in, the United States.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and post the revised policy on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: jianchaochi@gmail.com
  • Website: https://chineseenglishdict.com
